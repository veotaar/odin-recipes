Tofu Mayonnaise

Recipe and image from allrecipes.com by AMYJO107.

Description

This tofu mayonnaise is great for anyone who is lactose-intolerant or vegan. It's real thick and creamy and you can add seasonings to fit your fancy! Great for pasta salads or anywhere you'd use mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package tofu, drained and cut into cubes
  • ½ teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dry mustard
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon pepper
  • ¼ cup peanut oil

Steps

  1. Place the tofu, sesame oil, cider vinegar, mustard, salt and pepper into the container of a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Drizzle the peanut oil in a thin stream while blending until thick and pale. Use promptly.
